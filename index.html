About Me

Hi there, my name is Alyssa, and welcome to my portfolio!

Despite being relatively new to web development, I feel I've finally found the career that I was meant me. In no other profession have I found the perfect mix of analytic logic and accuracy, mixed with creativity in problem-solving and design. In the past, it has always felt like one or the other was neglected, or under-utilised until I found web development.

I have always actively sought ways to improve my knowledge and skillset, the challenge of which I enjoy immensely and intend to continue indefinitely, both for professional, and personal, benefit. Having a career that not only allows that, but almost demands it, feels like a gift.
